Our Story

Why we built this

Agriculture is one of the most important subjects for students in Papua New Guinea — yet many find it hard to go beyond memorising notes. MXT Lab was built to change that.

We focus on Grade 9 and Grade 10 Agriculture because that is where we can have the most direct impact on student outcomes. We cover all five strands — Crops, Animals, Aquaculture, Natural Resource Management, and Agribusiness — keeping every topic accurate, curriculum-aligned, and practical.

📢 A note on content: We update our content regularly and expand our topic library over time. All published lessons are complete and reviewed before going live.
