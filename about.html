About MXT Lab

MXT Lab is a free online Agriculture learning platform built specifically for Grade 9 and Grade 10 students in Papua New Guinea.

Our lessons cover all five strands of the PNG national Agriculture curriculum: Crops, Animals, Aquaculture, Natural Resource Management, and Agribusiness. Every topic includes structured lesson notes, key terms, a summary, and a knowledge check quiz.

MXT Lab was created by an Agriculture teacher at Kerema Coronation Secondary School in Gulf Province, Papua New Guinea — to give students access to quality, curriculum-aligned learning resources online.

Our Mission

To make quality Agriculture education accessible to every student in Papua New Guinea, regardless of where they live or what resources their school has.
