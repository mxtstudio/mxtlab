1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work properly, to remember your preferences, and to help website owners understand how their site is being used.

Cookies do not contain any information that directly identifies you as a person. However, some cookies are linked to personal information when combined with other data.

2. How MXT Lab Uses Cookies

MXT Lab uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the website functions correctly
  • To remember your preferences such as dark mode or light mode
  • To understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Can Be Disabled?
Essential Cookies Required for the website to function. These include cookies that remember your theme preference (dark or light mode). No — the site will not work correctly without them
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use MXT Lab — which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and where they come from. This helps us improve the site. Yes — through your browser settings
Advertising Cookies Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements based on your browsing history and interests. Yes — through Google's Ad Settings or your browser

4. Google AdSense Cookies

MXT Lab uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to show ads that are relevant to you based on your visits to this and other websites.

Google's advertising cookies include:

  • DoubleClick cookies — used to serve targeted advertisements across the web
  • Google Analytics cookies — used to track site usage and performance

You can opt out of Google's personalised advertising at any time by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses cookies, visit Google's Cookie Policy.

5. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to Google AdSense, MXT Lab may use third-party services that set their own cookies. These may include analytics tools and font providers such as Google Fonts. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings — most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Visit your browser's help section for instructions. Note that blocking some cookies may affect how MXT Lab works.
  • Google Ads Settings — visit google.com/settings/ads to manage how Google uses your data for advertising.
  • Your Online Choices — visit youronlinechoices.com to manage advertising cookies from multiple companies at once.

Please note that disabling advertising cookies does not mean you will stop seeing ads — it means the ads you see will be less relevant to your interests.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
