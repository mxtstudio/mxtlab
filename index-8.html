What You Will Study

5 Strands, both grades

Agriculture in Papua New Guinea is taught across five broad areas. MXT Lab covers all five for both Grade 9 and Grade 10.

🌾

Crops

Soil, crop types, farming practices, horticulture, and technology in crop production.

🐄

Animals

Monogastric and polygastric animals, poultry farming, and animal management systems.

🐟

Aquaculture

Freshwater, brackish, and saltwater farming of fish and aquatic plants.

🌳

Natural Resources

Forestry types, harvesting practices, forest management, and conservation.

📊

Agribusiness

Starting and managing an agribusiness, imports and exports, and business planning.

Our Approach

Learning through understanding

Agriculture is a practical subject. Our method is built around that reality — not just reading and memorising.

🧭

Explore the Concept

Each topic starts with a real farming question or problem. You think it through before we explain anything — building your own understanding first.

🔧

Work Through It

Explanations, diagrams, and practical examples help you work through the content step by step. You go at your own pace.

🪞

Check What You Know

Short questions and reflection tasks help you check your understanding before moving on — so nothing important gets missed.

About MXT Lab

Built for PNG
Agriculture students

MXT Lab is currently focused on one subject: Agriculture for Grade 9 and Grade 10 students in Papua New Guinea.

Our Story

Why we built this

Agriculture is one of the most important subjects for students in Papua New Guinea — yet many find it hard to go beyond memorising notes. MXT Lab was built to change that.

We are starting with Grade 9 and Grade 10 Agriculture because that is where we can have the most immediate impact. We cover all five strands — Crops, Animals, Aquaculture, Natural Resource Management, and Agribusiness — and we are building the content topic by topic, keeping everything accurate and practical.

We are a small team and we are still growing. We want to do Agriculture well before we consider expanding to other subjects.

📢 Honest note: MXT Lab is new. Not all topics are fully built yet — some are still being developed. We mark clearly which topics are available and which are coming soon. We will keep adding content regularly.
