Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the same content as the free lessons on the site?
The revision packs are a summarised, exam-focused version of the lesson content — condensed into key terms, diagrams and practice questions. Think of it as a study cheat sheet for the night before exams, not a replacement for reading the full lessons.
Can I print the PDF?
Yes. All revision packs are formatted for A4 printing. You can print them at home or at a local printing shop.
What if I don't receive my pack?
Message us on WhatsApp and we will resend. We aim to send all packs within 24 hours of payment confirmation.
Are the packs aligned to the PNG Agriculture curriculum?
Yes. All content is based on the Papua New Guinea Agriculture curriculum for Grade 9, 10, 11 and 12, following the five official strands: Crops, Animals, Aquaculture, Natural Resource Management, and Agribusiness.
Do teachers or schools get a discount?
Yes. Schools ordering packs for a whole class get a special rate. Message us on WhatsApp or visit the For Teachers page to learn more.
