1. Who We Are

MXT Lab ("we", "us", "our") is owned and operated by Brendan Kirori, based in Papua New Guinea. MXT Lab operates the website at https://mxtstudio.github.io/mxtlab/ — an interactive Agriculture learning platform for Grade 9 and Grade 10 students in Papua New Guinea.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at mxtlab.png@gmail.com.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate MXT Lab. This includes:

  • Information you give us voluntarily — such as your name and email address when you contact us through our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you use the site, such as which pages you visit. This is collected automatically by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and any analytics tools we use.
  • Cookies — small files stored on your device that help the site function and help us understand how it is being used. See Section 5 for more detail.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial details, government identification numbers, or medical information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to messages and enquiries you send us
  • Send you updates about new content on MXT Lab, if you have subscribed to our newsletter
  • Understand how users interact with the site so we can improve it
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense (see Section 6)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. How We Store & Protect Your Information

MXT Lab is hosted on GitHub Pages. Any personal information you submit through our contact form is transmitted securely. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

We retain your information only for as long as necessary. If you would like us to delete your data, please contact us at mxtlab.png@gmail.com.

5. Cookies

MXT Lab uses cookies to help the site function correctly and to understand how it is being used. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the site to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site, such as which pages are visited most often.
  • Advertising cookies — used by Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements based on your browsing. See Section 6.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect how parts of the site work.

6. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

MXT Lab uses Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to MXT Lab and other sites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you visit sites that use Google services, visit How Google uses data from sites that use our services.

7. Third-Party Links

MXT Lab may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

MXT Lab is designed for use by Grade 9 and Grade 10 students, some of whom may be under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at mxtlab.png@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask us what personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct any inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mxtlab.png@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of MXT Lab after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
