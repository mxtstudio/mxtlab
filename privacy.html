Privacy Policy

How MXT Lab collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our agriculture learning platform.

📅 Effective: 21 May 2025 🔄 Last updated: 21 May 2025 🌏 Papua New Guinea

This policy applies to mxtlab.online — a free agriculture learning resource for Grade 9 and Grade 10 students in Papua New Guinea. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

📋 1. Information We Collect

MXT Lab is primarily an informational and educational website. We collect limited data to operate and improve the platform:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type, and general geographic region, collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Contact form submissions — if you use the contact form, we collect your name, email address, and any message you provide.
  • Cookies — small data files placed on your device by us or our third-party partners (see Section 4).

We do not collect your full name, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily submit it through the contact form.

🎯 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the MXT Lab website
  • To understand how visitors use the site and improve content
  • To respond to contact form enquiries
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or abuse

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

🍪 3. Cookies

MXT Lab uses cookies to enhance your experience and enable core site functionality. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics cookies — placed by Google Analytics to help us understand site usage (pages visited, session duration, traffic sources).
  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense to display personalised or contextual advertisements. These may track your browsing activity across other websites.
  • Preference cookies — used to remember your dark/light mode preference during a session.

By continuing to use MXT Lab, you consent to our use of cookies as described above.

📢 4. Google AdSense & Advertising

MXT Lab uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to MXT Lab and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

📊 5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors use MXT Lab. This includes data such as which pages are most visited, how long visitors stay, and what devices are used.

Google Analytics uses cookies and collects your IP address, which may be used to estimate your general location (country or city level). This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

🔗 6. Third-Party Links

MXT Lab may contain links to external websites or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

👦 7. Children's Privacy

MXT Lab is an educational platform designed for secondary school students, including those under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The platform does not require account creation or login. If a parent or guardian believes their child has submitted personal information through our contact form and wishes to have it removed, please contact us using the details below.

🔒 8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information collected through MXT Lab. Contact form submissions are handled securely through Formspree. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

✏️ 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Continued use of MXT Lab after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
