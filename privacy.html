Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

MXT Lab (mxtlab.online) is a free educational resource providing Agriculture study materials for Grade 9 and Grade 10 students in Papua New Guinea. We are operated by MXT Lab, contactable at mxtlab.png@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

MXT Lab does not require you to create an account or log in. We do not collect personal data as a condition of accessing our content. If you contact us via the contact form, we collect your name and email address solely to respond to your message. We do not sell, share, or distribute this information to third parties.

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as page views) through standard web analytics to help us understand how the site is used and improve our content. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Cookies

MXT Lab uses no tracking cookies of its own. If third-party services such as Google Fonts or Google AdSense are active, those services may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Google AdSense

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

MXT Lab is designed for secondary school students aged 14 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

External Links

Our content may include links to external websites such as the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) or Papua New Guinea government resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us at mxtlab.png@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of MXT Lab after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at mxtlab.png@gmail.com.
