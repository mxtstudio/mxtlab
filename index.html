What You Will Study

5 Strands, all grades

Agriculture in Papua New Guinea is taught across five broad areas. MXT Lab covers all five for Grade 9, 10, 11 and 12.

Crops

Soil, crop types, farming practices, horticulture, and technology in crop production.

Animals

Monogastric and polygastric animals, poultry farming, and animal management systems.

Aquaculture

Freshwater, brackish, and saltwater farming of fish and aquatic plants.

Natural Resources

Forestry types, harvesting practices, forest management, and conservation.

Agribusiness

Starting and managing an agribusiness, imports and exports, and business planning.

Our Story

Why we built this

Agriculture is one of the most important subjects for students in Papua New Guinea — yet many find it hard to go beyond memorising notes. MXT Lab was built to change that.

We focus on Grade 9, 10, 11 and 12 Agriculture because that is where we can have the most direct impact on student outcomes. We cover all five strands — Crops, Animals, Aquaculture, Natural Resource Management, and Agribusiness — keeping every topic accurate, curriculum-aligned, and practical.

Future Pathways

Agriculture Careers

Discover where your Agriculture studies can take you. Explore real careers aligned to each strand of the PNG curriculum.

Agronomist

Crops

Agronomists advise farmers on soil health, crop varieties, fertiliser use, and pest management to improve yields sustainably. Coffee and cocoa — PNG's two biggest cash crops — rely heavily on agronomists.

Qualifications Bachelor in Sustainable Tropical Agriculture (BSTA) or BSc in Agriculture Institutions PNG UNRE (Kerevat, East New Britain); UNITECH — BSc Agriculture, Lae Employers NARI, Dept. of Agriculture & Livestock, Coffee Industry Corporation, NGOs

Agricultural Research Scientist

Crops

Research scientists develop improved crop varieties, study plant diseases, and find solutions to farming challenges. The National Agricultural Research Institute (NARI) leads this work in PNG.

Qualifications BSc Agriculture; postgraduate MSc, MPhil or PhD preferred Institutions UNITECH — BSc Ag, MSc Ag, MPhil & PhD (Lae); PNG UNRE (Kerevat) Employers NARI, international research bodies (CGIAR, FAO), universities

Agricultural Extension Officer

Crops

Extension officers work directly with rural farming communities, translating research findings into practical advice on crops, soil, and farming techniques. A critical role in PNG's rural provinces.

Qualifications Diploma or Bachelor in Agriculture; Bachelor of Agriculture and Rural Development (BARD) available by distance Institutions UNITECH — BARD distance mode (Lae); PNG UNRE (Kerevat); technical colleges Employers Dept. of Agriculture & Livestock, provincial governments, NGOs

Livestock Production Officer

Animals

Livestock officers manage the breeding, feeding, and health of farm animals including cattle, pigs, and poultry. They advise farmers on sustainable livestock production practices across PNG provinces.

Qualifications Bachelor in Sustainable Livestock Production (BSLP) or BSc Agriculture (Animal Science) Institutions PNG UNRE — BSLP (Kerevat); UNITECH — BSc Agriculture (Lae) Employers Dept. of Agriculture & Livestock, provincial governments, private farms

Veterinary Officer

Animals

Veterinary officers diagnose and treat disease in livestock, monitor animal health across farms, and enforce biosecurity regulations to protect PNG's food security and animal trade.

Qualifications Bachelor of Veterinary Science — PNG students typically study overseas as no full vet program exists locally Institutions James Cook University (Australia), University of the Philippines, Charles Sturt University (Australia) Employers Dept. of Agriculture & Livestock, National Agriculture Quarantine & Inspection Authority (NAQIA)

Poultry & Piggery Farm Manager

Animals

Farm managers oversee the day-to-day operations of poultry and pig farms — managing nutrition, disease prevention, breeding records, and production targets for commercial output.

Qualifications Diploma or Bachelor in Sustainable Livestock Production or BSc Agriculture Institutions PNG UNRE (Kerevat); UNITECH (Lae); technical vocational colleges Employers Private poultry farms, cooperatives, agribusiness companies

Fisheries & Aquaculture Officer

Aquaculture

Aquaculture officers manage fish farming systems in freshwater ponds and coastal areas. They monitor water quality, fish health, feeding, and harvesting for community and commercial fish farms.

Qualifications Bachelor of Sustainable Fisheries and Marine Resources (Aquaculture) Institutions PNG UNRE — Dept. of Sustainable Fisheries & Marine Resources (Kerevat); University of the South Pacific Employers National Fisheries Authority, Dept. of Fisheries, coastal communities

Marine Resources Scientist

Aquaculture

Marine scientists study ocean ecosystems, fish populations, coral reefs, and the impact of fishing on PNG's marine environment — one of the richest and most biodiverse in the world.

Qualifications Bachelor of Sustainable Fisheries and Marine Resources Institutions PNG UNRE (Kerevat); University of the South Pacific (Fiji/Samoa) Employers National Fisheries Authority, conservation NGOs, research institutes

Fisheries Inspector

Aquaculture

Fisheries inspectors enforce fishing regulations, monitor fish stocks, inspect commercial fishing vessels, and ensure compliance with PNG's fisheries laws to protect long-term fish supplies.

Qualifications Diploma or Bachelor in Sustainable Fisheries and Marine Resources Institutions PNG UNRE (Kerevat); University of the South Pacific Employers National Fisheries Authority of PNG

Forestry Officer

Natural Resources

Forestry officers manage PNG's vast forest resources — the third largest tropical rainforest in the world. They survey timber resources, monitor logging activity, and support reforestation efforts.

Qualifications Bachelor in Sustainable Tropical Forestry or BSc Forestry Institutions PNG UNRE (Kerevat); UNITECH — BSc Forestry with time at Bulolo Forestry College (Morobe); Lae Timber & Forestry Training College — diploma pathway Employers PNG Forest Authority, logging companies, conservation NGOs

Environment & Climate Change Officer

Natural Resources

These officers assess environmental impacts of development projects, monitor land and water health, and implement climate adaptation programs — an increasingly critical role as PNG faces climate change impacts.

Qualifications Bachelor in Environment and Climate Change Institutions PNG UNRE — School of Environment and Climate Change (Kerevat); UNITECH — BSc Applied Sciences (Lae) Employers Conservation & Environment Protection Authority (CEPA), mining companies, NGOs

Renewable Energy Engineer

Natural Resources

Renewable energy engineers design and install solar, hydro, and biomass energy systems — helping rural PNG communities access clean energy using natural resources sustainably.

Qualifications Bachelor in Renewable Energy and Environmental Engineering Institutions PNG UNRE — School of Environment and Climate Change (Kerevat); UNITECH — Engineering departments (Lae) Employers PNG Power, rural electrification projects, development organisations

Agribusiness Manager

Agribusiness

Agribusiness managers run the commercial and financial side of agricultural enterprises — planning budgets, managing supply chains, marketing produce, and overseeing business operations on farms and plantations.

Qualifications Bachelor of Sustainable Business in Accounting and Finance or BSc Business Studies Institutions PNG UNRE — School of Sustainable Resource Management and Business Studies (Kerevat); UNITECH — Dept. of Business Studies (Lae) Employers Plantations, export companies, cooperatives, banks

Commodity Export Officer

Agribusiness

Commodity export officers manage the buying, grading, and export of PNG's key agricultural products — especially coffee, cocoa, palm oil, and vanilla — ensuring quality standards for international markets.

Qualifications Bachelor in Sustainable Business or BSc Agriculture Institutions PNG UNRE (Kerevat); UNITECH — BSc Agriculture or Business Studies (Lae); UPNG — School of Business & Public Policy (Port Moresby) Employers Coffee Industry Corporation, Cocoa Board of PNG, export trading companies

Food Technologist

Agribusiness

Food technologists develop, test, and improve food products made from agricultural produce — turning raw crops and livestock into processed foods that meet safety, quality, and market standards.

Qualifications BSc Food Technology Institutions UNITECH — Dept. of Applied Sciences, BSc Food Technology (Lae); one of the few institutions in the Pacific offering this program Employers Food processing companies, PNG Cocoa Board, export firms, supermarket chains

Agricultural Tourism Coordinator

Agribusiness

Agricultural tourism coordinators develop eco-tourism and farm visit experiences that generate income for rural communities while showcasing PNG's unique natural resources and farming culture.

Qualifications Bachelor of Sustainable International Tourism Institutions PNG UNRE — School of Sustainable Resource Management and Business Studies (Kerevat) Employers Tourism Promotion Authority, eco-lodges, community enterprises
