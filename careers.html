Agriculture Careers

Career Pathways

Discover the wide range of careers available in Agriculture, Aquaculture, Forestry, and Agribusiness in Papua New Guinea.

Career Pathways

Discover the wide range of careers available in Agriculture, Aquaculture, Forestry, and Agribusiness in Papua New Guinea.

🌱

Agronomist

Crops

Agronomists advise farmers on soil health, crop varieties, fertiliser use, and pest management to improve yields sustainably. Coffee and cocoa — PNG's two biggest cash crops — rely heavily on agronomists.

QualificationsBachelor in Sustainable Tropical Agriculture (BSTA) or BSc in Agriculture
InstitutionsPNG UNRE (Kerevat); UNITECH — BSc Agriculture, Lae
EmployersNARI, Dept. of Agriculture & Livestock, Coffee Industry Corporation, NGOs
🔬

Agricultural Research Scientist

Crops

Research scientists develop improved crop varieties, study plant diseases, and find solutions to farming challenges. NARI leads this work in PNG.

QualificationsBSc Agriculture; postgraduate MSc, MPhil or PhD preferred
InstitutionsUNITECH — BSc Ag, MSc Ag; PNG UNRE (Kerevat)
EmployersNARI, international research bodies (CGIAR, FAO), universities
🌿

Agricultural Extension Officer

Crops

Extension officers work directly with rural farming communities, translating research into practical advice on crops, soil, and farming techniques. A critical role in PNG's rural provinces.

QualificationsDiploma or Bachelor in Agriculture; BARD available by distance
InstitutionsUNITECH — BARD distance mode (Lae); PNG UNRE; technical colleges
EmployersDept. of Agriculture & Livestock, provincial governments, NGOs
🍅

Horticulturalist

Crops

Horticulturalists specialise in growing fruits, vegetables, and ornamental plants. In PNG, this includes managing market gardens, spice crops, and export horticulture.

QualificationsCertificate or Diploma in Horticulture; BSc Agriculture (Crop Science)
InstitutionsPNG UNRE; Vudal University College; technical colleges
EmployersFresh Produce Development Agency (FPDA), private farms, export companies
🐄

Livestock Production Officer

Animals

Livestock officers manage the breeding, feeding, and health of farm animals including cattle, pigs, and poultry. They advise farmers on sustainable livestock production across PNG provinces.

QualificationsBachelor in Sustainable Livestock Production (BSLP) or BSc Agriculture (Animal Science)
InstitutionsPNG UNRE (Kerevat); UNITECH — BSc Agriculture (Lae)
EmployersDept. of Agriculture & Livestock, provincial governments, private farms
🐾

Veterinary Officer

Animals

Veterinary officers diagnose and treat disease in livestock, monitor animal health, and enforce biosecurity regulations to protect PNG's food security and animal trade.

QualificationsBachelor of Veterinary Science — PNG students typically study overseas
InstitutionsJames Cook University (Australia); University of the Philippines; Charles Sturt University
EmployersDept. of Agriculture & Livestock, NAQIA
🐔

Poultry & Piggery Farm Manager

Animals

Farm managers oversee day-to-day operations of poultry and pig farms — managing nutrition, disease prevention, breeding records, and production targets for commercial output.

QualificationsDiploma or Bachelor in Sustainable Livestock Production or BSc Agriculture
InstitutionsPNG UNRE (Kerevat); UNITECH (Lae); technical vocational colleges
EmployersPrivate poultry farms, cooperatives, agribusiness companies
🐝

Apiculturist (Beekeeper)

Animals

Apiculturists manage honeybee colonies to produce honey, beeswax, and pollination services. PNG has a growing apiculture industry with strong export potential for organic honey.

QualificationsCertificate in Agriculture or Apiculture; short courses through DAL
InstitutionsDept. of Agriculture training centres; PNG UNRE
EmployersPrivate beekeeping businesses, cooperatives, export companies, NGOs
🐟

Fisheries & Aquaculture Officer

Aquaculture

Aquaculture officers manage fish farming systems in freshwater and marine environments, advise on pond management, fish health, and help communities establish sustainable fish farms.

QualificationsBachelor in Sustainable Aquaculture (BSAq) or BSc Fisheries Science
InstitutionsPNG UNRE (Kerevat); UNITECH (Lae); UPNG
EmployersNational Fisheries Authority (NFA), Dept. of Agriculture, provincial governments
🌊

Marine Resources Scientist

Aquaculture

Marine scientists study ocean ecosystems, fish populations, and coral reef health. PNG has some of the world's richest marine biodiversity, making this a highly relevant career.

QualificationsBSc Marine Biology or Fisheries Science; postgraduate MSc preferred
InstitutionsUPNG; James Cook University (Australia); University of the South Pacific
EmployersNational Fisheries Authority, conservation organisations, international research bodies
🦐

Aquaculture Farm Operator

Aquaculture

Farm operators run freshwater or brackish water fish farms, managing feeding, water quality, harvesting, and sales. Tilapia and giant freshwater prawns are common species farmed in PNG.

QualificationsCertificate or Diploma in Aquaculture; hands-on training through NFA
InstitutionsNational Fisheries Authority training programs; PNG UNRE
EmployersPrivate fish farms, cooperatives, self-employment
🌲

Forestry Officer

Natural Resources

Forestry officers manage PNG's vast forest resources — overseeing sustainable logging, reforestation, and conservation programs. PNG has the third largest tropical rainforest in the world.

QualificationsBachelor of Forest Science (BFS) or BSc Natural Resource Management
InstitutionsPNG UNRE (Kerevat); UNITECH (Lae)
EmployersPNG Forest Authority, logging companies, conservation NGOs, provincial governments
♻️

Environmental Conservation Officer

Natural Resources

Conservation officers protect PNG's biodiversity — monitoring land use, enforcing environmental laws, and working with communities on sustainable resource management.

QualificationsBSc Environmental Science or Natural Resource Management
InstitutionsUPNG; PNG UNRE; UNITECH
EmployersCEPA, WWF, TNC, logging companies
🌍

Land Use Planner

Natural Resources

Land use planners assess how land should be used for farming, forestry, conservation, or development. They help balance economic growth with environmental sustainability in PNG provinces.

QualificationsBSc Natural Resource Management or Environmental Planning
InstitutionsUNITECH; UPNG; PNG UNRE
EmployersDept. of Lands, provincial governments, mining companies, NGOs
📊

Agribusiness Manager

Agribusiness

Agribusiness managers oversee the business side of agriculture — managing budgets, supply chains, marketing, and operations for farms, cooperatives, or agricultural companies in PNG.

QualificationsBachelor of Agribusiness Management or BBus (Agriculture)
InstitutionsPNG UNRE; UNITECH; Divine Word University (Madang)
EmployersCocoa Board, Coffee Industry Corporation, commodity export companies, banks
💰

Agricultural Finance Officer

Agribusiness

Agricultural finance officers assess and approve loans for farmers, evaluate farm business plans, and help rural communities access credit through banks and microfinance institutions.

QualificationsBBus (Finance) or Bachelor of Agribusiness with finance major
InstitutionsUNITECH; Divine Word University; PNG UNRE
EmployersBank South Pacific, Nationwide Microbank, Development Finance Corporation
🚚

Agricultural Supply Chain Officer

Agribusiness

Supply chain officers manage how agricultural products move from farm to market — coordinating transport, storage, processing, and export logistics for commodities like coffee, cocoa, and palm oil.

QualificationsBBus (Logistics) or Bachelor of Agribusiness Management
InstitutionsUNITECH; PNG UNRE; Divine Word University
EmployersNew Britain Palm Oil, commodity exporters, freight companies, cooperatives
📈

Agricultural Marketing Specialist

Agribusiness

Marketing specialists promote agricultural products to local and export markets, develop pricing strategies, and help farmers get better prices through improved market access.

QualificationsBBus (Marketing) or Bachelor of Agribusiness with marketing focus
InstitutionsUNITECH; Divine Word University; PNG UNRE
EmployersFresh Produce Development Agency, Coffee Industry Corporation, export companies
